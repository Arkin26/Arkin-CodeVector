Consistency demo

Start a browse session, simulate 50 writes to the live database, then load more. Duplicates should stay at 0 because your session is frozen at page 1.

How snapshot pagination works
  • Page 1 exports a PostgreSQL snapshot and freezes a point-in-time view.
  • Later pages import that snapshot and walk it with a keyset cursor on (updated_at, id).
  • New inserts/updates after page 1 do not reshuffle your session — no dupes, no misses.
